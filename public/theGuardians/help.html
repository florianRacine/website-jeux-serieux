The Guardians of Cybersecurity

Welcome to the thrilling world of The Guardians of Cybersecurity! Get ready to dive into an exciting game where you'll defend networks against digital threats and defeat the formidable Dark Knight.
  • Countdown : 6 minutes
  • You receive a hand of 4 cards (or 3 if playing with 6 players).
  • Each turn : you place a card and you draw a new card.
There are two types of cards.
  • White cards: be careful, the sum of the values in the corners of these cards must remain less than five.
  • The black cards: be careful the sum of the values in the corners of these cards must be lowered to less than five in the given time.
